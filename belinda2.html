My Story

My name is Belinda Melvin. I live in Chesapeake, Virginia. I've been married to a Navy veteran for over 22 years. We have 5 children, 3 dogs, a bird and 3 turtles. I've been in dentistry for over 30 years and it is my absolute passion!

I started out as a part-time administrative assistant at a dental practice, later became a clinical assistant to both the hygienist and dentist, along with assisting in patient scheduling. Later I transition to the treatment plan and insurance coordinator position. Shortly after I was promoted to practice manager.

Currently, I am the owner of BelMel Metrics, LLC, currently servicing clients across the United States. I believe in self-care, spending quality time with family and friends.
