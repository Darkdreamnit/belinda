About DentaBill Pro

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Sarah Johnson, a certified dental billing specialist with over 15 years of experience, DentaBill Pro was born out of a passion for helping dental practices optimize their revenue cycles.

After working in various dental offices and witnessing the challenges practices face with insurance claims and billing processes, Sarah dedicated herself to creating a service that would alleviate these pain points and allow dentists to focus on patient care.

Why Choose Us?

  • 15+ years of specialized dental billing experience
  • 98% first-pass claim acceptance rate
  • HIPAA-compliant processes to protect patient data
  • Personalized service tailored to your practice's needs
